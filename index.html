How to Make Beng Beng Cake

Simple and Easy

Make a cake with 4 ingredients

You will need this

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 4 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 beng beng pack

Tools

  • Steamer
  • Cake mold
  • Mixer
  • Bowl

Steps

  1. First crack the egg
  2. Then add 4 teaspoon sugar
  3. And stir it until foamy
  4. Then add beng beng powder
  5. And then add 2 tablespoon flour
  6. And stir it until foamy
  7. Afterthat, add 1 tablespoon margarine
  8. And stir it again
  9. And then pour it into the mold
  10. Steam it with low heat for 10 minutes
  11. After 10 minutes open the steamer
  12. And beng beng cake is ready to serve
